Our app

When learning's fun, why should it ever stop? Let your children learn the fun way with loads of games, activities, animated content, puppet shows that allow the child to develop their Cognitive, Literary, Socio-Emotional and Motor Skills

  • Story based animated content for the Age Group of 3-8 years(Early Schoolers) so that learning always remains a fun task
  • Content available in multiple languages including English and Indian Languages like Odia and Hindi.
  • Ability to function in areas with Zero Internet Connectivity
  • Content Based on National Education Policy(NEP) 2020 and Nua Arunima, Govt. of Odisha
  • Activity based worksheets that help students to capture their learnings and develop faster.
